Scan Pack (One-Time Purchase)

The Scan Pack is a one-time in-app purchase that provides 3 OCR receipt scan credits with no expiry date.

  • Scan credits do not expire — use them at any time.
  • Each purchased scan credit also grants 1 additional group creation slot beyond the free tier limit of 3 groups. With 3 scan credits, you may create up to 6 groups total.
  • As scan credits are used, the corresponding extra group slots are adjusted. The number of groups you can create equals your free tier limit plus your remaining scan credits.
  • Scan pack purchases are consumable — once scans are used, they are consumed and cannot be restored.
  • Multiple scan packs may be purchased to accumulate credits.
No refunds on used scan credits. Once an OCR scan has been processed (whether successfully or not due to image quality), that scan credit is consumed. Refund requests for unused scan packs must go through Google Play within the eligible window.

03

OCR Receipt Scanning & AI Processing

Version 2.0 introduces OCR receipt scanning. When you use this feature, your receipt image is processed as follows:

  • Tier 1 — On-device (Google ML Kit): The image is processed entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone. This is the primary processing method.
  • Tier 2 — OpenAI API (fallback only): If on-device processing yields insufficient results, the image is sent to OpenAI's API (GPT-4o-mini) for processing. The image is used solely to extract bill items and is not stored, trained on, or retained by us.
Important — Receipt images sent to OpenAI When Tier 2 processing is triggered, your receipt image is transmitted to OpenAI's servers. OpenAI's processing is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not store your receipt images on our servers at any point.

To avoid Tier 2 processing, ensure your receipt photos are clear, well-lit, and uncrumpled — Tier 1 on-device processing handles most clean receipts without any data leaving your device.

OCR scanning is a Pro feature or available via one-time scan pack purchase. The number of scans used per month is tracked in Firebase Firestore to enforce limits.

04

Firebase & Backend Services

Version 2.0 uses Firebase (by Google) for the following purposes:

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous or account-based authentication to associate your scan quota with your device.
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your scan usage count, purchased scan credits remaining, subscription plan type, and device fingerprint for abuse prevention.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Server-side validation of scan requests, scan pack purchases, and subscription status via Google Play Developer API.
Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Data stored in Firestore is used solely for scan quota management and purchase verification — not for advertising or analytics.

Your bill data, group names, member names, and settlement records are never sent to Firebase. Those remain exclusively on your device.

05

Local Storage & Data Loss Risk

All bill data (groups, items, members, settlements) is stored exclusively in the local Hive database on your device. This means:

  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all your groups, bills, and settlements.
  • Clearing app data or cache (via Android Settings → Apps) permanently deletes all data.
  • Switching to a new device will not transfer your data — there is no cloud backup for bill data.
  • Factory resetting your phone will erase all app data.
We strongly recommend using the Share Report feature (Settlement screen → share icon) or Export as CSV/PDF to save important data before uninstalling or changing devices.

07

Advertising (Free Users)

Free users may see interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. These ads are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalised ads.

Pro subscribers and scan pack buyers see no ads whatsoever.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via: Android Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation".

08

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

BillSplitMate offers two paid options via Google Play Billing:

  • Pro Subscription (monthly or yearly): Unlimited groups, members, items, OCR scans, PDF/CSV export, analytics, no ads.
  • Scan Pack (one-time purchase): 3 OCR receipt scans with no expiry. Each scan also unlocks 1 additional group slot beyond the free tier.

All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not store or have access to your payment card details. Purchase verification is performed server-side via Firebase Cloud Functions and the Google Play Developer API.

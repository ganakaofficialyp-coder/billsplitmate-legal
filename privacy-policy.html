01

Who We Are

BillSplitMate is developed and maintained by Yashp (Ganaka Official), an independent developer based in India. This Privacy Policy applies to the BillSplitMate Android application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

02

What Data We Collect

The App operates primarily offline. Here is a complete breakdown of every category of data involved:

Data Type Where It Goes Shared?
Group names, bill items, amounts, settlements Stored locally on your device only (Hive database) Never shared
Member names and phone numbers Stored locally on your device only Never shared
Contact data (if you use the contact picker) Only the selected contact's name and phone are saved locally. We never access your full contacts list. Never shared
Purchase / subscription data Handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We only receive confirmation of purchase status. Google Play only
Ad interaction data (free users only) Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalisation per its own privacy policy. Google AdMob only

We do not operate any servers or backend infrastructure. No data from your device is ever transmitted to us.

03

Local Storage & Data Loss Risk

All app data is stored exclusively in the local Hive database on your device. This means:

  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all your groups, bills, and settlements.
  • Clearing app data or cache (via Android Settings → Apps) permanently deletes all data.
  • Switching to a new device will not transfer your data — there is no cloud backup.
  • Factory resetting your phone will erase all app data.

We strongly recommend using the Share Report feature (Settlement screen → share icon) to export a PDF or text summary of important groups before uninstalling or changing devices.

04

App Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

Permission Why It's Needed Required?
READ_CONTACTS Allows you to pick a contact name and phone number when adding group members. Only the selected contact is accessed — never your full list. Optional
INTERNET Required to load ads (free users) and process in-app purchases via Google Play. Required

The contacts permission is entirely optional. You can add members by typing their names manually without ever granting contacts access. If denied, the contact picker feature will be unavailable but all other features work normally.

05

Advertising (Free Users)

Free users may see interstitial and banner ads served by Google AdMob. These ads are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalised ads.

Pro subscribers see no ads whatsoever.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via: Android Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation".

06

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

BillSplitMate Pro is offered as a monthly or yearly subscription via Google Play Billing. All payment processing, subscription management, and billing data is handled entirely by Google. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time via: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

07

Children's Privacy

BillSplitMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Significant changes will be communicated via an in-app notice where reasonably practicable.
